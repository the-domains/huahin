Hua Hin

Thai Boxing Garden is a venue that hosts Muay Thai Boxing fights and training camps. Muay Thai Boxing is Thailand's national sport and is played and taught from a young age. The sport has gained huge popularity worldwide due to the growth in mixed martial arts. Thai Boxing Garden has fights every Tuesday and Saturday from 9pm. The format is normally 5 fights per night and the bouts range from kids to women to different men's weight divisions. Thai Boxing Garden is also open from 7am until 7pm every day to offer Muay Thai Boxing training. the training is an excellent way to keep fit and learn how to defend yourself. The owner, Mr Chop will make you feel welcome and organise training sessions tailored to your age and level.
